## Portfolio Themes

### Theme 1: AI-Native Vertical Software (10/16 companies - 63%)
Actuals, Apollo, Chirp, Elita, Fluency, Keeyu, Kindling, Shopfront + AI elements in Flux and Puralink.

The dominant pattern. Each company applies AI/automation to a specific, underserved operational pain point in a vertical. This is the core investment thesis.

### Theme 2: Space & Defense (3/16 companies - 19%)
Aquila, HEO, MP Space.

A deliberate cluster betting on Australia's growing sovereign space and defense capability. Differentiator from typical early-stage AU investors.

### Theme 3: Robotics & Hard Tech (4/16 companies - 25%)
Flux (farm robots), Puralink (pipe robots), Aquila (laser systems), MP Space (spacecraft hardware).

Willingness to back companies building physical products with deep engineering, not just software.

### Theme 4: Physical Infrastructure Impact
Flux (farming), Puralink (pipes), Magic Valley (food supply), Aquila (energy), HEO + MP Space (space).

Companies solving tangible, real-world infrastructure problems.

### Theme 5: Consumer / Health / Lifestyle (smaller cluster)
Elita (pet health), Magic Valley (cultivated meat), Metahuman Labs (supplements).

### Theme 6: Ecosystem Play
Aussie Angels - a platform to syndicate angel investment itself, complementing the broader portfolio.
