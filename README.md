# Davis Enterprises Holdings - Website Redesign Project

A comprehensive analysis and redesign brief for [davisenterprises.com.au](https://davisenterprises.com.au/), informed by competitive research across 9 comparable early-stage and deep tech venture funds.

## What's In This Repo

### 1. [REDESIGN_BRIEF.md](./REDESIGN_BRIEF.md)
The main deliverable. A two-column redesign proposal where:
- **Left column:** What was changed and why (with evidence from competitive analysis)
- **Right column:** Proposed structure, copy direction, and layout for each section

Includes a full page wireframe, design recommendations, and priority-ranked action items.

### 2. [COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md](./COMPETITIVE_ANALYSIS.md)
Deep analysis of 9 fund websites that compete for the same type of deal flow:
- Main Sequence (CSIRO deep tech)
- Blackbird Ventures (largest ANZ VC)
- AirTree Ventures (network-powered ANZ VC)
- Square Peg Capital (APAC concentrated bets)
- Tenacious Ventures (agrifood-tech specialist)
- Skip Capital (Atlassian family office)
- Lux Capital (US deep tech)
- In-Q-Tel (US intelligence community VC)
- Breakthrough Victoria (state sovereign fund)

Covers: hero messaging patterns, language/tone analysis, positioning angles, target audience breakdown, homepage structure patterns, trust signals, and social proof tactics.

### 3. [PORTFOLIO_ANALYSIS.md](./PORTFOLIO_ANALYSIS.md)
Analysis of all 16 Davis Enterprises portfolio companies, organized by theme:
- AI-Native Vertical Software (10/16 companies)
- Space & Defense (3/16)
- Robotics & Deep Tech (4/16)
- Consumer / Health / Lifestyle
- Ecosystem plays

## Key Findings

**What the best fund websites do (that Davis should adopt):**
1. Lead with a bold, single-line belief statement (not "Welcome")
2. Show portfolio with one-line descriptors (not just logos)
3. Put a founder-facing CTA ("Pitch Us") in the navigation
4. Name their thesis pillars explicitly (not "sector agnostic")
5. Show the investor's face and bio on the homepage
6. Include founder testimonials about specific value-add
7. Offer direct email contact (not LinkedIn-only)
8. Publish lightweight thought leadership content

**What no good fund website does:**
- Lead with fund size or returns
- Use corporate jargon
- Use aggressive sales language
- Hide behind animations instead of content

## How to Use This

This repo is designed to be handed to a designer or developer as a brief. The `REDESIGN_BRIEF.md` contains everything needed to understand the "what" and "why" behind each proposed change. The competitive analysis and portfolio analysis provide the supporting evidence.
