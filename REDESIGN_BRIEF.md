# Davis Enterprises Holdings - Website Redesign Brief

## Prepared by: Design & CRO Advisory Team
## Date: February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

We analyzed 9 comparable early-stage / deep tech funds (Main Sequence, Blackbird, AirTree, Square Peg, Tenacious Ventures, Skip Capital, Lux Capital, In-Q-Tel, Breakthrough Victoria) to identify what the best-performing fund websites do to attract high-quality deal flow from founders.

The current davisenterprises.com.au site has strong visual design (dark theme, animated elements, clean portfolio grid) but misses several critical patterns that 7-9 out of 9 comparable funds use to convert founder interest into inbound deal flow.

This brief presents a section-by-section redesign recommendation in a two-column format: **left column explains what changed and why**, **right column shows the proposed structure/content**.

## Design Recommendations

### Keep from current site:
- **Dark theme** - works well for tech/deep tech positioning. Main Sequence, Lux, and Blackbird all use dark themes.
- **Clean typography** - the serif/sans-serif pairing (Cormorant Garamond + Outfit) is sophisticated.
- **Accent color (#2d7fff / blue)** - professional and tech-forward.

### Change:
- **Reduce animations** - the current scan lines, floating orbs, and glitch effects are visually impressive but can slow page load and distract from content. None of the 9 analyzed funds use heavy animation. Clean transitions only.
- **Increase whitespace** - let the content breathe. The current site packs elements tightly. The best fund sites (Blackbird, Skip, Lux) use generous whitespace.
- **Simplify visual effects** - remove the grid background overlay, noise texture, and scan line effects. They add visual complexity without communicating anything about the fund.
- **Mobile optimization** - ensure the portfolio grid stacks cleanly on mobile. Most founder-investor first contact now happens on mobile (shared via text/LinkedIn DM).

### Typography:
- Keep Cormorant Garamond for headlines (distinctive, literary)
- Keep Outfit for body text (clean, readable)
- Keep JetBrains Mono for accents (tech credibility)

### Color:
- Keep the dark base (#040810)
- Consider warming the accent slightly - current #2d7fff is good, but a slightly warmer tone (#4da8ff) could feel more approachable while maintaining tech credibility

## Priority Actions (Ranked by Impact)

1. **Rewrite the hero** - single bold statement, no "Welcome" (highest impact, lowest effort)
2. **Add one-line descriptors to portfolio companies** (highest conversion impact)
3. **Add "Pitch Us" CTA to navigation** (direct conversion path)
4. **Create thesis pillars section** (helps founders self-select for fit)
5. **Add Peter's bio to homepage** (builds trust for a personal fund)
6. **Source 3 founder testimonials** (social proof from portfolio)
7. **Add direct email contact** (reduce friction vs LinkedIn-only)
8. **Reduce visual effects/animation** (improve load time and readability)
9. **Start a lightweight journal** (thought leadership, SEO, deal flow)
10. **Add newsletter signup** (community building)
